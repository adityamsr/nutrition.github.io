Here are impressive health benefits of kidney beans 


1. Kidney beans are an excellent plant-based source of protein. They're also rich in 
   various minerals, vitamins, fibers, antioxidants, and other unique plant compounds.
   Therefore, these beans may aid weight loss, promote colon health, and moderate blood
   sugar levels.

2. Canned beans are healthy as long as you're selecting varieties that are simply beans.
   No unhealthy ingredients like sugar or salt (sodium chloride) have been added. Canned
   beans that are simply beans (no sodium, sugar, or other harmful ingredients added)
   are a superbly healthy food choice.
